The accident occurred on March 11, 2011, when an earthquake in Japan triggered a tsunami that shut down all four nuclear power plants. Cooling system failure due to loss of power, and thus heat cannot be discharged in time. The emergency cooling system that was activated also failed, resulting in seawater not being able to enter the reactor to bring out the heat. Excessive heat causes equipment melting and radiation release.
